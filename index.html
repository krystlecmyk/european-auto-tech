About Us

Ben, Cory, and Shelly are all Nebraska natives that make up the European Auto Tech team. Ben opened European Auto Tech in 2012 to fulfill his lifetime goal. Ben and Cory are BMW and Mercedes factory trained and started their careers in 2001 at the local BMW dealership and worked together for seven years. They have spent the majority of their careers with BMW at the dealership level locally and on the west coast. Ben recruited Cory in 2014, and Shelly came on board in 2013 to complete the European Auto Tech Team.

With a combined 40+ years experience in automotive repair we have the knowledge, training, and experience to repair and maintain your vehicle!
